Refund Policy

We strive to provide the best service possible to our customers. However, in the event that you are unsatisfied with your purchase or experience a failed transaction through our app resulting in a deduction from your bank account, we offer the following refund policy:

1. Refund Process:

  • Requests for refunds must be made within 7-14 days in normal, and 30-45 days to rare case of the original transaction date.
  • To initiate a refund, please contact our customer support team at info.dsd47@gmail.com.
  • Upon receiving your refund request, we will review the transaction details and assess eligibility for a refund.

2. Refund Eligibility:

  • Refunds will only be issued for failed transactions resulting in deductions from your bank account.
  • Refunds will not be granted for completed transactions or instances where the deduction is from your wallet balance.
  • In case of refunds for wallet deductions, the refund amount will be deducted from your wallet balance.

3. Evidence Requirement:

  • For refunds related to failed transactions, you may be required to provide evidence such as transaction receipts or bank statements to support your claim.
  • Failure to provide adequate evidence may result in the denial of your refund request.

4. Refund Processing Time:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-14 days in normal, and 30-45 days to rare case upon approval of the refund request.
  • Please note that the refund amount may take additional time to reflect in your bank account or wallet, depending on the payment method used.

5. Right to Refuse Refunds:

  • We reserve the right to refuse refunds if we determine that the request does not meet the eligibility criteria outlined in this policy.
  • Refunds may also be denied if there is evidence of misuse or abuse of our services.

By using our services and making transactions through our app, you agree to abide by the terms and conditions of this refund policy. We reserve the right to modify or update this policy at any time without prior notice. For any further inquiries or assistance regarding refunds, please reach out to our customer support team.
